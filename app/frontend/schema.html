Architecture Notes

Bronze layer: Raw scraped data with minimal transformation. Prices, sales, and categories are normalized but not enriched.

Cross-platform matching: Products are matched via CLIP image embeddings (vector cosine similarity ≥0.70) + title keyword overlap. Matches are 1-to-1 deduped.

Taxonomy: 3-level hierarchy (L1/L2/L3) via pattern-matching on titles against a curated taxonomy tree.

Sales estimation: Amazon "bought in past month" text parsed (5K+ → 5000). ML from listing metadata. US uses BSR estimation.

Migration pattern: We push product data + denormalized matches to the client DB. Client receives clean data via source_key (platform:platform_id) for linking. Our internal columns (embedding, image_hash, timestamps) never leave our system.

Sync cadence: synced_at on destination tracks when each product was last refreshed. Delta syncs compare last_updated vs synced_at.
