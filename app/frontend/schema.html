Architecture Notes

Bronze layer: Raw scraped data with minimal transformation. Prices, sales, and categories are normalized but not enriched.

Cross-platform matching: Products are matched via CLIP image embeddings (vector cosine similarity ≥0.70 threshold) + title keyword overlap. Matches are 1-to-1 deduped.

Taxonomy: 3-level hierarchy (L1/L2/L3) assigned via pattern-matching on titles against a curated taxonomy tree. L1 = department, L2 = group, L3 = type.

Sales estimation: Amazon "bought in past month" text is parsed (5K+ → 5000). ML sales come from listing metadata. Amazon US uses BSR-based estimation when direct sales are unavailable.

Soft delete: is_active = false archives products without removing data. All active queries filter on is_active = TRUE.
